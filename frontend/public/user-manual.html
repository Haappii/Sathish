1. Getting Started

Registration

Visit haappiibilling.in/setup/onboard to register your business. You'll receive:

  • Shop ID — Your unique shop identifier
  • Username — Default is "admin"
  • Temporary Password — You'll be asked to change it on first login

All credentials are sent to your registered email. Your shop comes with a 30-day free trial with all features unlocked.

Login

Go to haappiibilling.in/login and enter your Shop ID, Username, and Password. On first login, you'll be prompted to set a new password.

Available Platforms

🌐 Web App

Access from any browser at haappiibilling.in

💻 Windows Desktop

Dedicated counter app with offline support

📱 Android App

Mobile billing, printing & operations on the go

🖨️ Thermal Printing

Bluetooth & WiFi thermal receipt printing

2. Dashboard & Home

The home screen shows your shop name, branch, business date, and online/offline status. Quick action tiles give one-tap access to all features.

Trial Period

During your free trial, a badge in the header shows remaining days. When the trial expires, contact the Haappii Billing team to activate a subscription plan.

Business Date

The business date controls which day's transactions you're working on. It's set by the admin and can differ from the calendar date (useful for shops that operate past midnight).

Theme

Switch between Light, Dark, or System theme from the sidebar menu or settings.

3. Sales Billing

Creating a Bill

  1. Go to Sales Billing from the home menu
  2. Select items from the category grid or search by name
  3. Adjust quantities using +/- buttons
  4. Enter customer name and mobile number (optional)
  5. Select payment mode: Cash, Card, UPI, Credit, Gift Card, Coupon, Split, or Wallet
  6. Click Save & Print to create the invoice and print receipt

Payment Modes

ModeDescription
CashStandard cash payment
CardDebit/credit card payment
UPIGoogle Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.
CreditRecorded as due — customer pays later
Gift CardRedeem a gift card code at checkout
CouponApply discount coupon
SplitSplit payment across multiple modes
WalletPay from customer's stored wallet balance

Billing History

View all past invoices filtered by date range (Today, 7 Days, 30 Days). From here you can:

  • View invoice details
  • Print/reprint receipts
  • Edit invoice (modify items, customer, payment mode)
  • Delete invoice (admin only)
  • Share invoice via WhatsApp

GST Billing

Enable GST from Setup → Shop Details. Configure:

  • GST Number — Your GSTIN
  • GST Percentage — Default tax rate
  • GST Mode — Inclusive (price includes tax) or Exclusive (tax added on top)
  • HSN Codes — Set per item for detailed GST reporting
Tip: Use the Hold feature to save a bill in progress and serve the next customer. Resume held bills from the Draft Bills section.

4. Table Billing (Hotels & Restaurants)

Table Management

Set up tables from Setup → Tables. Each table has a name, capacity, and status (Available, Running, Reserved).

Order Flow

  1. Open the Table Billing screen to see the table grid
  2. Tap a table to start/view an order
  3. Add items from the menu
  4. Send to kitchen (generates KOT — Kitchen Order Ticket)
  5. Add more items as needed
  6. When ready, generate the final bill and collect payment

KOT (Kitchen Order Ticket)

KOT management shows all pending kitchen orders. Kitchen staff can mark items as prepared. Track order status in real-time from the Order Live screen.

QR Ordering

Generate QR codes for each table. Customers scan the QR code, browse the menu on their phone, and place orders directly. Orders appear in the QR Orders section for staff to accept.

Takeaway

Create takeaway orders without assigning a table. These function like regular bills but are tagged as takeaway for reporting.

Recipe Management

Define recipes for menu items — link raw materials with quantities. When a dish is sold, raw material stock is automatically deducted based on the recipe.

5. Inventory Management

Items

Add items from Setup → Items with:

  • Item name, category, HSN code
  • Selling price, buy price, MRP
  • Unit (kg, g, ml, L, pcs)
  • Minimum stock level (for alerts)
  • Item image
  • GST rate per item

Branch-wise Pricing

Enable Items Branch Wise from settings to set different prices for each branch. If not enabled, all branches share the same item prices.

Stock Tracking

View current stock levels from the Inventory screen. Stock is automatically adjusted when:

  • A bill is created (stock decreases)
  • A bill is deleted (stock restored)
  • A purchase order is received (stock increases)
  • A return is processed (stock restored)
  • A stock audit is performed (manual adjustment)

Stock Audit

Perform physical stock counts and reconcile differences with system stock. Adjustments are logged for accountability.

Stock Transfers

Transfer stock between branches. Create a transfer request, specify items and quantities, and the receiving branch confirms receipt.

Item Lots (FIFO)

Track purchase lots with different costs. When selling, the system can use FIFO (First In, First Out) costing for accurate profit calculation.

6. Customers & Dues

Customer Management

Customers are automatically created when you bill with a name and mobile number. View all customers, their purchase history, and outstanding dues from the Customers section.

Dues & Receivables

When a bill is created with Credit payment mode, it's recorded as a due. Track and collect dues from the Dues section. Record partial payments and view remaining balances.

Customer Wallet

Customers can maintain a wallet balance. Add money to the wallet, and customers can pay from their wallet at checkout.

7. Employees & Attendance

Employee Management

Add employees with name, role, contact, salary, and joining date. Track employee settlements and advances.

Attendance

Mark daily attendance for employees. View attendance reports by month. Supports Present, Absent, Half-day, and Leave statuses.

8. Expenses

Track all business expenses — rent, electricity, salaries, supplies, etc. Each expense has a date, category, amount, and optional notes. View expense summaries in reports.

10. Loyalty, Gift Cards & Coupons

Loyalty Program

Set up a points-based loyalty system. Customers earn points per purchase and redeem them for discounts. Configure points-per-rupee and redemption value.

Gift Cards

Create and sell gift cards with custom amounts. Gift cards have unique codes and optional expiry dates. Customers redeem at checkout by entering the code.

Coupons

Create discount coupons — flat amount or percentage. Set validity dates, minimum order value, and usage limits. Customers apply coupon codes during billing.

11. Purchase Orders & Suppliers

Suppliers

Manage your supplier database with contact details, GST numbers, and payment terms. Track supplier ledger (payments in/out).

Purchase Orders

Create POs for suppliers with item lists, quantities, and prices. Track PO status: Draft → Ordered → Partially Received → Received. Receiving a PO automatically updates inventory stock.

Supplier Ledger

View complete payment history with each supplier. Track outstanding payables and record payments.

12. Advance Orders

Accept advance bookings with partial payment. Track order status, delivery date, and remaining balance. Convert advance orders to regular invoices when fulfilled.

13. Cash Drawer

Track physical cash in the register. Open the drawer with a starting amount, record cash in/out during the day, and close with denomination count at end of day. The system calculates expected vs actual cash for reconciliation.

14. Shop Setup & Configuration

Shop Details

Configure shop name, address, logo, GST settings, and contact information. This appears on printed receipts and invoices.

Branches

Add multiple branches under one shop. Each branch has its own address, inventory, and staff. The head office branch manages all branches.

Categories

Organize items into categories for easy navigation during billing. Categories appear as tabs on the billing screen.

Items

Add products/menu items with prices, images, and stock settings. Supports bulk import via Excel upload.

Users

Create login accounts for staff with assigned roles (Admin, Manager, Cashier, Waiter). Each user can be assigned to a specific branch.

Tables

Configure tables for hotel/restaurant billing. Set table names, sections, and capacity.

16. Printing & Thermal Printers

Supported Printers

  • Bluetooth Thermal Printers — Connect via Bluetooth for silent ESC/POS printing (58mm/80mm)
  • WiFi/Network Printers — Connect to network printers via IP address
  • System Print Dialog — Use any printer connected to your PC

Setup (Mobile App)

  1. Go to Settings → Printer Settings
  2. Tap Scan Bluetooth to discover paired devices
  3. Select your thermal printer from the list
  4. Toggle Direct Thermal Printing to ON
  5. Save settings
Tip: The app auto-detects paired Bluetooth printers on launch. If a printer is found and none is configured, it will be automatically selected.

Receipt Content

Receipts include: shop name, address, GST number, invoice number, date, items with quantities and prices, tax breakdown, payment mode, and a QR code for digital invoice access.

18. Offline Mode

The app works offline — bills created without internet are queued and automatically synced when connectivity returns.

  • Items and categories are cached locally
  • Bills are stored in the offline queue
  • A banner shows the number of pending bills
  • Tap the banner to manually trigger sync
  • The system checks connectivity every 15 seconds
Note: Sign in online at least once before using offline mode. Offline login uses cached credentials.

19. WhatsApp Integration

Send invoices to customers via WhatsApp automatically after billing. Requires Meta WhatsApp Business API setup.

Features

  • Send invoice PDF as a WhatsApp document
  • Send invoice link as a text message (fallback)
  • Advance order receipts via WhatsApp
  • Per-branch enable/disable toggle

Enable WhatsApp

Go to Settings on the mobile app or web app and toggle WhatsApp Invoice to enabled. The admin must configure the WhatsApp API credentials on the server.

20. Security & Data Privacy

  • Encrypted passwords — All passwords are bcrypt-hashed
  • JWT authentication — Secure token-based sessions
  • Single session — Only one active login per user at a time
  • Session timeout — Auto-logout after inactivity
  • HTTPS only — All data encrypted in transit
  • Biometric lock — PIN/fingerprint on mobile app
  • Audit trail — All actions are logged with timestamps
  • Role-based access — Users only see what their role permits

21. FAQ & Troubleshooting

I forgot my password

Contact your shop admin to reset your password from Setup → Users.

Bills are not syncing

Check your internet connection. Go to Offline Sync and tap the sync button. If bills fail repeatedly, check if your session has expired and re-login.

Printer not found

Ensure Bluetooth is ON and the printer is paired in your phone's Bluetooth settings first. Then scan from the app's Printer Settings.

GST not showing on bills

Enable GST from Setup → Shop Details. Set the GST percentage and mode (inclusive/exclusive). Ensure individual items have GST rates assigned.

Can't access certain features

Your user role may not have permission. Ask the admin to check Setup → Permissions and enable the required modules for your role.

Trial expired

Contact the Haappii Billing team at haappiibilling@gmail.com or +91 79042 63246 to activate a subscription plan.

How to delete an invoice?

Only admins can delete invoices. Go to Billing History, open the invoice, and click Delete. Deleted invoices are moved to the Deleted Invoices section for audit purposes.
