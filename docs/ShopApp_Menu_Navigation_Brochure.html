Quick Navigation Map

After login, users land on Home. The left sidebar (role-based) is the primary navigation menu.

Home

Main dashboard, shortcuts, sales snapshots, and daily business highlights.

Sales

Billing, billing history, edits, table billing, QR orders, and KOT (hotel mode).

Inventory and Stock

Inventory, stock audit, transfers, item lots, labels/barcodes, and reorder tracking.

Operations

Dues, returns, expenses, customers, online orders, support tickets, and offline sync.

People and Loyalty

Employees, attendance, loyalty, gift cards, and coupons.

Reports and Setup

Reports, deleted invoices, and full setup/admin configuration pages.

Simple Daily Flow

1) Start day: Login -> Home -> check alerts and stock.
2) Billing: Open Billing / Table Billing -> create bills -> print/share.
3) Midday control: Monitor Dues, Expenses, Online Orders, Inventory updates.
4) End of day: Use Reports + Day Close for final check.
5) Admin updates: Setup module for items, users, branches, suppliers, and permissions.
